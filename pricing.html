FAQ
Common questions.
Do I need a credit card to start? +
No. The free plan is completely free with no credit card required. You can use Compyte as long as you want on the free tier.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. Cancel anytime from your settings. No questions asked, no penalties.
Is my financial data safe? +
Yes. 256-bit AES encryption. Data stored on AWS Mumbai (ap-south-1). Row-level security — only you can see your company's data. We never sell your data.
Is Compyte a replacement for my CA? +
Compyte handles financial visibility, compliance tracking, and AI-powered guidance. It is not a licensed CA or financial advisor. We recommend using Compyte alongside your CA — you'll save them hours every month.
Do you offer refunds? +
Yes. If you're unsatisfied within 7 days of any charge, email cnxfam@gmail.com for a full refund.
Can my CA or investor access Compyte? +
Yes. On Starter and Pro plans, you can invite team members with role-based access. CA and Investor roles have read-only access to relevant financial data.
